Step by step:

  • Step 1: Acquire an NFT shoe from the in-app Market or utilize the shoe rental feature.
  • Step 2: Equip your NFT Shoe and join beFITTER activities (Walk/Run/Cycle).
  • Step 3: Medal(s) will be randomly distributed at the end of your valid activity sessions (exp-earned sessions).
  • Step 4: Exp Point equals 1 Energy, and Medal(s) will be randomly distributed at the end of your valid activity sessions
  • Step 5: In beFITTER application, click Tournament Tab from your Homepage, claim your Medal(s) as your Free Pass Reward according to your level in the tournament. (Level in the tournament is decided by the Exp Points you have accumulated). You will start from level 0 and try to reach the highest level (level 30).
  • Step 6: Unlock Premium Pass with 25,000 $HEE to enter the Leaderboard, earn as many Medal(s) and Gacha Box(es) as your Premium Pass Reward and climb up the Leaderboard..

Conditions:

  • Only users who use NFT Shoes (including Rental Shoes) to do activities will be given Medals and/or Exp Point(s) to level up in the tournament.
  • Does not apply to the Trial Shoes.
  • Exp Point(s) can only be earned by consuming Energy (integer value).
  • Medal(s) will be randomly distributed at the end of valid activity sessions. A valid activity session means the session in which exp has been earned. The Distribution Rate is decided by the Luck Attribute of the NFT Shoe.
  • Participants need to unlock the Premium Pass to join the Leaderboard and compete to get HEE rewards from the Pay Fund.
